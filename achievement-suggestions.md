# Achievement Suggestions

Shortlisted new achievement ideas for Tennis Table.

## 1. Anniversary 🎂
Play a game exactly 1 year (±1 day) after your first ever game.

## 2. Century Club 💯
Play 100 total games.

## 3. Deuce Marathon 🏓
Win a set 15-13 or higher (a long deuce battle).

## 4. Free Fall 📉
Drop 300 Score from your all-time high.

## 5. Conqueror 🗺️
Beat every currently ranked active player at least once.

## 6. Cursed 🪦
Lose to every currently ranked active player at least once.

## 7. Throne Holder 👑
Hold rank #1 for 30 consecutive days.

## 8. Dynasty 🏛️
Hold rank #1 for 90 consecutive days.

## 9. Rocket 🚀
Climb 100 Score in a single calendar week.

## 10. Yin Yang ☯️
Alternate Win/Loss/Win/Loss for 10 games straight.

## 11. Perfect Day ☀️
Go undefeated across 5+ games in a single day.

## 12. Worst Day 🌧️
Lose 5+ games in a single day without a win.

## 13. Tour de Table 🚲
Play against 5 different opponents in a single day.

## 14. Pyrrhic Victory 💀
Win a game by the smallest possible margin — you actually lost more total points than your opponent across all sets.

## 15. King Maker 🤝
Be the player from whom the new #1 has gained the most Score on their climb to the throne. Awarded retroactively when a player reaches rank #1, to the opponent who contributed the most Score gain to that climb.

## 16. Late Bloomer 🌷
Enter a season's top 3 only in its final week.

## 17. Streak Ender 🛑
Beat a player who was on an active 10+ game win streak.

## 18. Founding Member 🪴
Be among the first 5 players ever created in the league.

## 19. Block Party 🎉
Play on a day where every currently active ranked player also plays.

## 20. Group Stage Star ⭐
Go undefeated in a tournament group stage.
